# CLAUDE.md — Level 3: Gastrointestinal System

## Setting
Inside the GI tract. Sub-levels: oesophagus → stomach → small intestine → colon.
Partially fluid environment in the stomach sub-level.

## Unique Mechanic
Peristalsis — the floor moves. Direction varies by sub-level (forward in intestine, retrograde in reflux).

## Enemies
- **Henchmen:** E. coli
- **Boss:** C. difficile
- H. pylori appears as a mini-boss in the oesophagus/stomach sub-level

## Obstacles
| Obstacle | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Peristalsis | Floor scrolls — player must move with or against it |
| Reflux | Backflow pushes player toward start |
| Ulceration | Blood spurts from floor cavities |
| Food bolus | Large obstacle approaching from behind |
| Diarrhoea | Fast forward flow — difficult to control |
| Wind | Burst of speed forward |

## Antibiotic Unlocks
- Triple therapy combo (Amoxicillin + Clarithromycin + PPI) — H. pylori reward
- Metronidazole (C. diff / anaerobe cover — boss reward)
- PPI unlocked as a special weapon for PUD sub-level

## Hints
- H. pylori triple therapy
- C. diff treatment (metronidazole → vancomycin → fidaxomicin escalation)
- E. coli (preview for Level 4)
- Loperamide and ondansetron as supportive tools
