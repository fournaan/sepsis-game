# CLAUDE.md — Level 4: Urinary System

## Setting
Underwater world inside the urinary tract. Sub-levels: urethra → bladder → ureter → kidney.
Visibility degrades progressively: clear → haematuria (red) → pyuria (cloudy).

## Unique Mechanic
Visibility as a difficulty modifier. Later sub-levels are near-opaque.

## Enemies
- **Henchmen:** E. coli (returning from Level 3)
- **Boss:** Pseudomonas aeruginosa

## Obstacles
| Obstacle | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Haematuria / Pyuria | Reduced visibility |
| Urethra walls | Wall-kick climbing required to ascend |
| Kidney stones | Small debris projectiles to dodge |
| Hydronephrosis | Dilated, flooded chamber — swim mechanic |
| Ureteric stent | Narrow corridor obstacle |

## Antibiotic Unlocks
- Trimethoprim / Nitrofurantoin (uncomplicated UTI — early reward)
- Ciprofloxacin (pyelonephritis / Pseudomonas cover — boss reward)
- Alpha blocker unlockable for BPH sub-level (non-antibiotic tool)

## Hints
- Urine dipstick findings (nitrites, leucocytes, blood)
- UTI vs pyelonephritis vs urosepsis distinction
- E. coli as the most common uropathogen
- Pseudomonas: when to suspect, what covers it
