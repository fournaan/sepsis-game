# CLAUDE.md — Level 2: Respiratory System

## Setting
Inside the respiratory tract. Levels progress from nasal cavity → pharynx → bronchi → alveoli.
Air resistance increases with depth. Windy throughout.

## Unique Mechanic
Airflow as a constant environmental force — direction and strength vary by sub-level.

## Enemies
- **Henchmen:** H. influenzae
- **Boss:** TB (Mycobacterium tuberculosis — requires atypical cover)

## Obstacles
| Obstacle | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Sneeze | Short burst of strong forward air resistance |
| Cough | Multiple bursts of air resistance |
| Rhinorrhoea | Mucus slide pushes player back; dodge via floating objects |
| Smoking | Screen blackout for short period |
| Mucus plug | Tight gap — player must duck |
| Supplemental O2 | Air push from behind — speed boost |

## Antibiotic Unlocks
- Amoxicillin / Doxycycline (H. influenzae — henchman reward)
- Rifampicin + Isoniazid combination (TB — boss reward)

## Hints
- Air pressure changes down the respiratory tract
- Typical vs atypical respiratory pathogens
- TB treatment regimen (RIPE therapy)
- Antibiotic coverage chart unlock
